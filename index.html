About Squid Qatar Project ?
🦑Welcome to Squid Qatar 🦑 👏 Congratulations to Qatar for hosting the FIFA world cup 2022 with the exciting atmosphere of the biggest football festival on the planet approaches.
🤔 But then the joyful atmosphere is the sad story of more than 6700 migrant workers from the brother country who lost their lives in the process of preparing for the FIFA World Cup 2022.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 To thank and remember these labor heroes, we've created a decentralized World Cup code token geared towards co-values and charity fundraising.
